Neighbours are family

Condominiums have common areas. With Condo-me, residents can enjoy these areas in an organized, respectful, and equitable manner. Through rules predefined by an administrator user, each resident can schedule, reschedule, and cancel specific dates and times for using each common area, and all users will have the transparency that will provide the well-being so important for collective living.

Why Condo-me?

Do it yourself

There is no need to request assistance from condominium staff to make or manage your reservations.

Schedule

Choose the common area you want to use and make a reservation in a couple of steps.

Availability

Check if the common area you want to schedule is available.

Cancel

Won't be able to use the common area? No worries, canceling a reservation is easy.

Get reminders

The app can send you reminders as your reservation approaches.

Search

With Condo-me you may check for past dates reservations you made.

Rules

Understand the reservation rules and also the terms of use for the common areas of your condominium.
